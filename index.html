Who we are?


"The Connection Ministry is a vital arm of the church focused on fostering meaningful relationships and ensuring that every individual who walks through the doors feels welcomed, supported, and integrated into the life of the church. This ministry is dedicated to building a strong sense of community by connecting people to the church’s resources, groups, and opportunities for spiritual growth. It serves as the bridge between newcomers, regular attendees, and the broader church community, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to engage, belong, and thrive within the church environment. Through intentional follow-up, ongoing support, and the creation of welcoming spaces, the Connection Department/Ministry plays a key role in helping individuals move from initial contact to active participation and discipleship."
